AI Overview ⋮
Weight loss injections are prescription medications that regulate appetite, available on the NHS (with strict eligibility criteria) or through private registered pharmacies. Three main once-weekly injections are approved in the UK: Mounjaro (tirzepatide), Wegovy (semaglutide), and Saxenda (liraglutide). B British Heart Foundation +4
1. The Approved Injections
Mounjaro (Tirzepatide): A highly effective dual-action once-weekly injection that mimics two gut hormones to curb appetite and cravings. It is available privately or on the NHS for eligible patients.
Wegovy (Semaglutide): A popular once-weekly GLP-1 injection clinically proven to significantly reduce appetite and prevent cardiovascular events like strokes.
Saxenda (Liraglutide): A daily GLP-1 injection. It is one of the older approved options but is generally less effective for weight loss than Mounjaro or Wegovy. Bo Boots Online Doctor +5
2. How to Access Them
Through the NHS
You must meet specific criteria regarding Body Mass Index (BMI) and have weight-related health conditions (like type 2 diabetes or heart disease).
You generally need a referral from your GP to a specialist weight-management service.
Due to high demand and NHS funding, access is rolling out gradually, and waiting lists can be long. N NHS England +4
Through Private Prescribers
You can get these medications privately via online registered doctor services if you meet their clinical safety criteria.
Costs: Typically range from £150 to £300+ per month depending on the medication and the dosage required.
To buy safely, use Care Quality Commission (CQC) regulated providers such as Boots Online Doctor or Superdrug Online Doctor. Bo Boots Online Doctor +4
3. Safety and Use
These medications are not a "quick fix" and are specifically designed to be used alongside a reduced-calorie diet and increased physical activity.
Common side effects include nausea, vomiting, and diarrhoea.
Always consult your GP or a qualified medical professional before starting any weight loss treatment to ensure it is safe for you. Bo Boots Online Doctor +4
