Lululemon accepts returns of unwashed, unworn merchandise with original hangtags attached for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Proof of payment is required. Markdown or "We Made Too Much" items, along with swimwear (if hygienic liners are removed), are considered final sale. lululemon UK +3
Key details of the return policy include:
Return Options
In-Store: You can return items to any local Lululemon store for a quick and free return, or to process an exchange.
By Mail: You can initiate an online return via the Lululemon Returns Portal using your order number to generate a prepaid shipping label or QR code. lululemon UK +3
Important Conditions
Condition: Items must be in brand-new condition with all original tags attached.
Excluded Items: Gift cards, items bought at outlets, and "final sale" (markdown) products cannot be returned.
No Receipt? If you lost your receipt, you can provide your email or phone number so they can attempt to locate your guest profile. Returns without proof of payment may be applied to a Lululemon gift card at their discretion. lululemon UK +3
