AI Overview⋮
To measure curtains correctly, first measure the width of your curtain pole or track, then multiply by 1.5–2.5 for fullness. For the drop, measure from the pole to where you want the curtain to end — floor, sill, or just below. D dunelm.com
A breakdown of the key measurements you need:
Width: Measure the pole or track from end to end (not the window). Multiply by 1.5 for a light gather, or 2–2.5 for a full, luxurious look.
Drop (Length): Measure from the top of the pole or track down to your chosen finish point — sill, below sill, or floor. Add 15–20 cm for hems if making your own.
Drop Length Options
Sill length: Measure from the pole to the bottom of the window sill. Good for kitchens and bathrooms.
Below sill: Add 10–15 cm below the sill for a classic look that clears radiators.
Floor length: Measure from the pole to the floor, then subtract 1–2 cm to avoid dragging. D dunelm.com
Puddle length: Add 10–20 cm to the floor measurement for a dramatic, formal style.
Tip: Always measure each window individually — windows in the same room are rarely identical. Use a metal tape measure for accuracy. D dunelm.com
