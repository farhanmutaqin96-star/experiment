AI Overview⋮
Nordstrom offers birthday perks through its loyalty program, The Nordy Club. Members receive surprise birthday offers, which usually include a voucher to spend or bonus reward points. To secure these benefits, you must update your date of birth in your Nordstrom Account Profile at least 30 days before your birthday month. Nordstrom +3
The specific birthday reward depends on your Nordy Club tier and whether you shop at Nordstrom or Nordstrom Rack:
Standard Members: Receive a "Personal Double Points Day" to use on a date of your choosing during your birthday month, plus access to beauty perks (such as exclusive bonus points on beauty purchases).
Cardmembers: If you carry a Nordstrom credit or debit card, you may receive a special birthday bonus — often in the form of a $10–$20 "Nordstrom Note" (a spending voucher) or bonus points deposited into your account.
Nordstrom Rack App: If you use the Nordstrom Rack App, you will frequently find a surprise digital birthday perk waiting in the app during your special month. Nordstrom Rack +4
Key Rules to Know
30-Day Rule: Your birthday must be saved in your account profile at least 30 days prior to the start of your birthday month to qualify.
Expiration: Birthday reward Notes and points come with an expiration date — check the exact date in the email or your account dashboard when it is issued.
Where to Shop: Standard Nordstrom Notes can be used at Nordstrom, Nordstrom.com, Nordstrom Rack, and Nordstrom Local locations. Nordstrom +2
